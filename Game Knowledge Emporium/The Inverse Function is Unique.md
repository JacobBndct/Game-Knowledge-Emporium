*If $h: Y \rightarrow X$ and $k: Y \rightarrow X$ are both inverses of the function $f: X \rightarrow Y$ then $h = k$.*

*Proof:* We compute as follows (see below for further explanations): $$h = h \circ id_Y = h \c$$
