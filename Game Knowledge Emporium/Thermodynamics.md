Thermodynamics: macroscopic description of the behavior of large numbers of particles, in particular concerning the interaction of heat and (mechanical) work

Important macroscopic parameters: 
- pressure: p
- volume: V
- temperature: T

**Mass**
- m (kg)

**Volume**
- V ($m^3$)

**amount: the mole**
- n (mol)

**Energy**
- E (J = kg $m^2 s^{-2}$)

**Pressure**
- p (Pa = $N/m^2$)

**Temperature**
- T (K)
