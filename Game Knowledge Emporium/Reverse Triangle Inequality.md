**Reverse Triangle Inequality:**
$$||x| - |y|| \le |x - y|$$
**Proof:**
$$\begin{align} |x| = |x - y + y| \le |x- y| + |y| \\ \\ |x| - |y| \le |x-y| \\ \\ |y| - |x| \le |y - x| = |x - y| \;\;\; \text{swap x and y} \\ \\ ||x| - |y|| = max\{|x| - |y|, |y| - |x|\} \le |x-y|\end{align}$$
